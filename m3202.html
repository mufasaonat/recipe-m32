YUMMY YAMMY GREEN TEA TIRAMISU RECIPE

This delicious and unbelievably easy Green tea Tiramisu recipe is made with green tea soaked lady fingers, sweet and creamy mascarpone (no raw eggs!), and cocoa powder dusted on top. It requires no baking and can be made in advance!

Who doesnt love an easy no-bake dessert? Thats one of the best things about this tiramisu recipe. If you are looking quick no-bake dessert

Tiramisu is not only delicous but also easy to make! like i said it requires no bake but instead requires cooling

What make this recipe special? This recipe requires to egg which makes tiramisu tastes better in any way!

  • Mix the creamy filling Beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar, and vanilla together until stiff peaks.
