Tiramisu

This delicious and unbelievably easy Tiramisu recipe is made with coffee soaked lady fingers, sweet and creamy mascarpone, and cocoa powder dusted on top. It requires no baking and can be made in advance!

Ingredients

  • Savoiardi or Lady Finger
  • Heavy creamy
  • Eggs
  • Mascarpone (Or can use Cream cheese instead)
  • Sugar
  • Espresso
  • Cacao powder

How to do Tiramisu

  1. Beat Heavy Cream with sugar until it peak. Put Mascarpone in cream, And continue beating.
  2. Beat egg yolk with sugar until the sugar dissolve.
  3. Mix cream and egg yolk.
  4. Soak Lady Finger in Espresso.
  5. Put soaked lady fingers in container and put cream on top. Do this for 2-3 layer.
  6. Sprinkle cocao powder
