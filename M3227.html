Strawberry cheesecake

This strawberry cheesecake is a graham cracker crust and creamy filling with swirls of sweetened strawberry sauce. It's perfect for any special occasion!

Strawberry cheesecake have 3 main part

1 Cheesecake biscuit base

2 Cheesecake filling

3 Strawberry sauce
