Stir-fried squid with salted eggs Recipe And How to do.


Popular menu items such as “Stir-fried squid with salted eggs” doesn't have to go to a seafood restaurant. It's easy to make and eat at home. If you want to know how to do it, Let's come and see!

Recipe

  • 4 salted egg yolks
  • ⅓ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon chili paste
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar.
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 350 grams of sliced squid
  • 1 stalk of celery, cut into pieces
  • 2 spring onions, cut into pieces.
  • Red chilies cut into strips for decoration.
  • Vegetable oil

How To Do??

    STEP 1 : Make salted egg sauce.

  • Put salted egg yolks into a bowl. Follow with water, grind well, then add chili paste, oyster sauce, soy sauce,
    and sugar. Stir well and set aside.

    • STEP 2 : Stir-fried squid.

  • Place the pan on the stove over medium heat. When the pan is hot, add the vegetable oil. Followed by chopped garlic
    Add the prepared crushed egg yolks and stir. Followed by sliced squid. Salted egg, cut into pieces Stir-fry until the squid is cooked. And the salted egg yolk coats the squid all over.

    • STEP 3 : Serve.

  • Add celery, scallions and chili peppers. Stir to combine. Then scoop it up and put it on a plate. Garnish with red chilies, sliced into strips. It's ready to serve.
