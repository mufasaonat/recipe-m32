Kaeng Som

Ingredients for Kangsom

1. 4 liters of water

2.Boiled sea bass, meat only 500 grams

3.Lemongrass

4.Peeled fingerroot 100 grams

5.Peeled shallots 80 grams

6.Ready-made curry paste 320 grams

Step to do it

1.Bring clean water to a boil, dissolve Knorr Chicken Soup, stir until dissolved.

2.Add galangal, shallots and fish and boil until cooked. Separate and blend until smooth.

3.Put the curry paste and blended fish into a pot and bring to a boil.

4.Bring clean water to a boil, dissolve Knorr Chicken Soup, stir until dissolved.

5.Add mixed vegetables as desired. Simmer until the soup is thick. Serve hot. Add meat as desired, such as fried fish or fresh river prawns.
