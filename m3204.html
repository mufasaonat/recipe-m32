history

Stir Fry Pork With Basil or PradKarPaoMu is popular dish in Thailand know for its taste and how easy to make it nomally serve with rice.It originated around the reign of King Rama VII because Chinese people started selling it in restaurants.PradKarPao appear on many street food and resterunt in Thailand

Recipe

Pound garlic and dried chili together. Heat a pan with oil, turn on the gas stove over medium heat, then add the pounded chili and garlic and fry until fragrant. Then add the minced meat and stir-fry until the meat is cooked slightly. Then add oyster sauce, fish sauce, sweet dark soy sauce, ground pepper as desired and stir-fry until the meat is cooked. Taste and adjust the seasoning again to get the desired flavor. Then add the basil leaves, dried chili, stir-fry together and it is ready to eat.
