How to make donut

Homemade donuts, recipe from Khun Kongkan, a member of the Pantip.com website. The donuts are made from wheat flour mixed with sweetened fresh milk, butter, sugar, and yeast.
They are fried until cooked and then coated with icing.

Ingredients

  • 120 grams of granulated sugar
  • Yeast 15 grams5
  • 100 ml water
  • 140 ml sweetened fresh milk
  • 5 grams of salt
  • Chicken eggs (size 50-55 grams) 2 eggs
  • All-purpose flour 500 grams
  • 30 grams of unsalted butter
  • Soybean Fat (for frying donuts)

How to make

  • Mix sugar, yeast, water, sweetened fresh milk, salt, eggs, all-purpose flour and unsalted butter in a mixing bowl.
    Knead by hand for about 40 minutes, then let it rest for 30 minutes until the dough has doubled in size.
  • Take the dough that has risen and pound it to release the air and let it rest for another 30 minutes or until the dough has doubled in size.
  • Roll the dough into a 1 cm thick layer. Then, use the bottom of a glass and a cookie cutter to press the dough and let the donut dough rise.
  • Put soybean fat for frying donuts in a pan or pot. Use medium heat to fry until both sides are white and fluffy.
    Then, let it turn yellow one side at a time. This will give you white edges. Scoop it up and drain the oil.
